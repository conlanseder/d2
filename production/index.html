Owner's Project Management

About

Dan Dunwoodie, PMP

As a Project Manager for D Squared Projects, Dan is an effective communicator with an ability to develop strong, professional relationships with clients, consultants, government personnel and contractors. His clients value the insight he brings to their projects, and the time and money saved by helping them to avoid the numerous development pitfalls.

With over 13 years of construction and development management experience under his belt, Dan set out to create D Squared Projects. The business objective was simple – provide first-class, professional and knowledgeable project management services to companies that don’t have the time, resources or know-how to self-perform.

Services

We work in the Owner’s interest. It is our duty to manage the Architect, Engineers, Contractor and other stakeholders to align and protect this interest.

Some of the specific tasks we manage are:

  • Requirements Gathering
  • Facility Planning
  • Determining Project Delivery Method
  • Assembling the Design and Engineering Team
  • Design
  • Drawing Review
  • Value Engineering
  • Municipal Approvals
  • Multi-Stage Budgeting
  • Detailed Scheduling
  • Construction Contract Tender
  • Change Management
  • Team Meetings
  • Organizing Project Documents
  • Construction Field Reviews
  • Reporting to Stakeholders
